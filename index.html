The firewall — two halves, one wall

Diabetes is a 24/7 job. We built the tools to carry it — without ever taking your data. OpenDiabetic and LocalDiabetic have opposite invariants, and that opposition is the whole design.

🐝 OpenDiabetic — the hive

Donated & owned compute. Fine-tunes the open models, hosts them, earns income renting to research and business.

A private vault in your own home. Reminders to your phone & watch, an on-box AI that organizes your records and preps your doctor visits.
