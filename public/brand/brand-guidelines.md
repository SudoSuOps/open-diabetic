# OpenDiabetic Brand Assets

OpenDiabetic uses a warm bee mark with a two-tone wordmark:

- `Open` uses deep teal: `#073f3b`
- `Diabetic` uses honey gold: `#c47b00`
- Primary accent honey: `#f2b632`
- Primary infrastructure teal: `#0f766e`
- Soft background: `#f8fbf7`

## Files

- `/brand/opendiabetic-mark.svg` - square mark for avatars and app icons.
- `/brand/opendiabetic-logo-horizontal.svg` - primary horizontal logo.
- `/brand/opendiabetic-logo-stacked.svg` - stacked logo for social, documents, and slides.
- `/favicon.svg` - browser icon.
- `/og-image.svg` - social sharing preview.

## Usage

Use the bee mark as a warm public-good signature. Keep the wordmark serious and legible. Do not use the bee in a way that makes medical claims, implies diagnosis, or turns the brand into a childish healthcare app.

Minimum clear space around the mark should be roughly one half of the mark width. Prefer the horizontal logo in headers and the stacked logo in documents or social cards.
