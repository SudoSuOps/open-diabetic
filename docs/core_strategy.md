# OpenDiabetic Foundation Core Strategy

OpenDiabetic Foundation is a privacy-first diabetic compute foundation.

It does not exist only to publish diabetes content, sell supplies, or build another diabetes app. It exists to own and operate the compute layer that powers DiabeticOS, DiabeticMOS, LocalDiabetic edge deployments, diabetes research workloads, developer toolkits, AI models and agents, local diabetic data vaults, Apple Watch/iPhone notification workflows, NAS-based personal health records, lifestyle support systems, and care pack/local resource coordination.

## Core Principle

We do not harvest diabetic data.

OpenDiabetic should be designed around local ownership, local storage, user consent, and privacy-first compute. The user's data should live in their personal vault whenever possible: NAS, edge appliance, encrypted local store, or user-controlled cloud backup.

The compute layer should serve the user, the family, the researcher, the developer, and the diabetic community without turning diabetic data into the business model.

## Positioning

OpenDiabetic Foundation provides diabetic compute as public-good infrastructure.

### Compute for Diabetics

- Run local reminders
- Organize records
- Generate appointment prep
- Manage supply lists
- Support Apple Watch notifications
- Coordinate care circles
- Maintain emergency sheets
- Summarize user-owned records locally
- Help simplify day-to-day diabetic life

### Compute for Research

- Support privacy-preserving research workloads
- Support open datasets where appropriate
- Support synthetic and de-identified data workflows
- Support model evaluation for diabetic education and support tools
- Support community research grants or compute credits

### Compute for Developers

- Provide APIs and toolkits for diabetic support apps
- Provide safe AI agent templates
- Provide DiabeticOS modules
- Provide local-first storage patterns
- Provide evaluation harnesses
- Provide compliance and safety guardrails

### Compute for AI Models and Agents

- Diabetic lifestyle agents
- Reminder agents
- Records organizer agents
- Supply inventory agents
- Caregiver update agents
- Local resource agents
- Research assistant agents
- Document explanation agents
- Non-diagnostic education retrieval agents

### Compute for Local Communities

- LocalDiabetic nodes
- Care pack routing
- Vendor verification
- Volunteer workflows
- Clinic/community resource coordination
- Local donation matching
- Local supply logistics

## Product Doctrine

DiabeticOS is the lifestyle operating system for living with diabetes. It focuses on daily simplicity: what needs to happen today, what supplies are low, what appointments are coming, who needs an update, what documents are needed, what should be packed, and what support can be requested.

DiabeticMOS is the management operating system around the person. It coordinates records, reminders, support circles, care packs, vetted vendors, medical contacts, insurance documents, local resources, family updates, post-hospital recovery, volunteer support, donation workflows, and privacy permissions.

LocalDiabetic is the local deployment layer. It supports NAS personal vaults, optional Jetson/local AI appliances, Apple Watch/iPhone alerts, local-first records, family permissions, emergency access, local vendor lists, support networks, optional cloud backup, and no data harvesting.
